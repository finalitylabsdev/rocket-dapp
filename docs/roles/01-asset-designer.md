# 1. What Is This Project?

## 1.1 The Product in 60 Seconds

Entropy is a **blockchain‚Äëbased gaming platform** with four web apps:

1. **Entropy Gate** ‚Äî Users lock real money (ETH) and receive a token called **œÜ (Entropy)** every 24 hours.
2. **Entropy Exchange** ‚Äî A trading hub where users swap œÜ for other tokens.
3. **Star Vault & Nebula Bids** ‚Äî Users buy **mystery boxes** with œÜ. Each box contains a random **NFT rocket part** with stats and a rarity tier. Rare parts can be **auctioned** for more œÜ.
4. **Celestial Assembler & Quantum Lift‚ÄëOff** ‚Äî Users assemble rockets from 8 parts, launch them, get a score ("Grav Score"), and compete on a **leaderboard** for real money prizes.

**Your assets bring this universe to life.** The parts, boxes, rockets, events, and visual effects are the emotional core of the product.

## 1.2 The Universe

The Entropy universe takes place in a **stylised deep space** setting. Not hard sci‚Äëfi realism, not cartoon. Think: **a premium mobile game's art direction meets editorial illustration** ‚Äî clean, detailed enough to feel crafted, stylised enough to feel unique.

**Reference mood:**

| Avoid                              | Aim For                                                            |
| ---------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Realistic NASA renders             | Stylised, slightly abstracted space ‚Äî luminous, jewel‚Äëlike.        |
| Flat vector clip‚Äëart               | Rich detail with controlled complexity.                            |
| Over‚Äëdetailed pixel art            | Clean silhouettes that read at small sizes (80px card thumbnails). |
| Generic crypto/DeFi aesthetics     | Something that looks like it belongs in a premium game world.      |
| Bright, saturated "kiddie" colours | Deep, moody palette with selective neon pops.                      |

## 2.2 Art Style Guide

### General Style

* **Render style:** Semi‚Äërealistic with stylised lighting. Think: Destiny 2 inventory icons, or high‚Äëend mobile game UI art.
* **Lighting:** Dramatic, single‚Äësource, with bloom on high‚Äërarity items. Light source comes from upper‚Äëleft by default.
* **Materials:** Metal, glass, energy fields, crystalline structures. All futuristic. No wood, stone, or organic textures.
* **Linework:** Clean edges. No rough/sketchy lines. High‚Äërarity items may have an energy aura that softens edges.
* **Detail level:** Enough to look beautiful at 400px; still readable at 80px. Silhouettes must be distinct.

### Colour Application in Art

* **Base tones:** Use the `--void` to `--dust` range for dark areas and backgrounds within the asset.
* **Metal highlights:** Use `--star` and `--star-dim` for metallic reflections.
* **Rarity integration:** The rarity colour should appear as the dominant glow, energy field, accent lighting, or material tint on the part. It should feel intrinsic to the part, not like a coloured overlay slapped on top.
* **Section identity:** Each of the 8 rocket sections should have a subtle visual language that distinguishes it (e.g., engines have exhaust nozzles, wings have aerodynamic curves, shielding has layered plates). More on this in Section 4.
